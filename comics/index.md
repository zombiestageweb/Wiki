Alongside the video Rounds, VIVINOS publishes a **Zombie Stage comic**. This page tracks the
official source and community translation efforts.

## Official comic

- **Patreon:** [patreon.com/vivinos](https://www.patreon.com/vivinos)
  Primary release channel for Zombie Stage comic pages, previews, and behind-the-scenes.

## Community translations

Fan translations are collected here. Please support the official source — subscribe or tip on
Patreon if you can.

- **English translations:** community posts on the [Alien Stage subreddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/AlienStage/) and Alien Stage Facebook groups.
- **Spanish (Español):** community translations circulate in Alien Stage Discord servers and Twitter/X.

If you maintain a translation set, [add it via PR](https://github.com/zombiestageweb/Wiki/edit/main/comics/index.md).
