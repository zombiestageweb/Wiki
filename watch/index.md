Zombie Stage is released **officially and for free** by VIVINOS. This page collects the legitimate
channels — we do **not** link to unauthorized re-uploads.

## Official channels

- **YouTube — VIVINOS:** [youtube.com/@vivinos](https://www.youtube.com/@vivinos)
  Primary release channel for every Round.
- **Patreon — VIVINOS:** [patreon.com/vivinos](https://www.patreon.com/vivinos)
  Comic pages, early previews, behind-the-scenes.
- **Fandom (encyclopedic reference):** [ALIEN STAGE Wiki](https://alienstage.fandom.com/)

## Is Zombie Stage on Netflix / streaming?

**No.** Zombie Stage is a creator-owned web series released directly on YouTube. It is not on
Netflix, Crunchyroll, Disney+, or any subscription platform. Anyone claiming otherwise is either
mistaken or hosting a re-upload.

## Watch order

1. Watch mainline **Alien Stage (Round 1 → KARMA)** first — it establishes the cast.
2. Then jump into **Zombie Stage Round 1**.
3. Keep an eye on this wiki and VIVINOS' YouTube for Round 2 onward.
