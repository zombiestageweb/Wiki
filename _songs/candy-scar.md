**Candy Scar** is a song from **Zombie Stage Round 1**. It anchors **Sua's** arc in the Round — the moment
the "Sewer" nickname takes on its full weight, and Sua's fixation on Mizi curdles into the macabre
finale that Round 1 is now infamous for.

## Meaning (community reading)

"Candy" and "scar" together frame the AU's core tension: something sweet, marketable, and
consumable — layered over the physical damage the arena inflicts on its performers. Sua's version
of the song reads as both love song and eulogy.
