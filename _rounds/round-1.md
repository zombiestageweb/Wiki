**Round 1** is the opening act of Zombie Stage and centers on **Mizi** and **Sua**. It reintroduces
the two mainline-ALNST leads inside the Zombie Stage AU, then delivers the AU's mission-statement
moment: the on-stage dismemberment and post-match auction that defines every Round to follow.

## What happens in Round 1

Flashbacks show how Mizi and Sua both ended up in Zombie Stage. Mizi, still riding her K-pop idol
image, is captured and zombified but kept "presentable" enough to keep performing. Sua — the
"Sewer" of Zombie Stage — is drawn in behind her, and the pair are matched against each other in
the first Round.

The Round is a **dark critique of the cutthroat idol industry**: Mizi's fame is what keeps her
alive and staged; Sua's obsession with Mizi is what keeps her there at all. The ending is the
tragic, macabre auction sequence that fans have not stopped talking about since drop.

## Round 1 ending explained

Round 1 ends with the loser being **dismembered on stage** and their body parts auctioned to the
audience. This is not a metaphor within the AU — it is the fixed format of Zombie Stage matches.
The scene reframes every song that came before it: the performance was never for the performers,
it was inventory prep.
