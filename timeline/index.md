Zombie Stage is an **alternate universe**. It is not a sequel to *Alien Stage*'s KARMA ending and
does not overwrite mainline canon. Below is the current placement.

## Mainline Alien Stage (canon)

Round 1 → Round 2 → Round 3 → Round 5 → TOP 3 → Round 6 → **Final Round (KARMA)** → *Wiege* (epilogue).

Mainline ALNST is a **finished arc**. Everything after KARMA is either supplementary material
(Wiege, sub-songs, comics) or AU content.

## Zombie Stage (AU)

Round 1 (June 2026) → Round 2 (TBA) → Round 3 (TBA) → Round 4 (TBA) → Round 5 (TBA) → Round 6 (TBA).

Zombie Stage begins its own Round count from 1 and does not share continuity with mainline ALNST.
Characters and relationships carry over in **archetype**, but events do not.

## FAQ

**Do I need to watch mainline Alien Stage first?**
Strongly recommended. The AU's punch depends on you already caring about Mizi, Sua, and the rest.

**Is Zombie Stage post-KARMA?**
No. It is a separate branch, not a continuation.

**Will there be crossover episodes?**
Nothing has been announced.
