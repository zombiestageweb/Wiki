**Zombie Stage is an official alternate universe of the [*Alien Stage*](https://alienstage.fandom.com/) (ALNST) series by [VIVINOS](https://www.youtube.com/@vivinos).** Round 1 released in **June 2026**. Instead of aliens hunting humans, humans have turned into zombies — and the *non-infected* minority run an underground arena where infected performers sing for their survival.

## The premise in one paragraph

Zombies aren't eradicated in this AU; they're **commodified**. Wealthy non-infected humans run an
underground, circus-like venue where zombified performers are matched against each other in
Rounds. Losers are dismembered on stage and their body parts are auctioned to "fans." It is a
zombie musical, an idol satire, and a horror show — at the same time.

## How Zombie Stage differs from mainline Alien Stage

|                        | Alien Stage (mainline)                       | Zombie Stage (AU)                                   |
|------------------------|----------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------|
| Threat                 | Aliens vs. humans                            | Non-infected humans vs. zombified humans            |
| Performers             | Human contestants                            | Zombified idols / performers                        |
| Loser fate             | Killed                                       | Dismembered, body parts auctioned                   |
| Tone                   | Sci-fi tragedy                               | Horror carnival / idol satire                       |
| Structure              | Rounds 1 → Final (Karma)                     | Round 1 → Round 6 (planned)                         |

## Is Zombie Stage a zombie musical?

Functionally, yes. It is a serialized set of music videos in which zombified characters perform
staged numbers as part of an in-universe tournament. Its closest tonal neighbors are *Alien
Stage* itself, VIVINOS' earlier work, and the horror-musical tradition of shows like
*Repo! The Genetic Opera*.

## Is Alien Stage finished after Karma? Where does Zombie Stage fit?

Mainline *Alien Stage* concluded its main arc with **KARMA** (Final Round). **Zombie Stage is an
AU**, not a sequel — it reuses the cast and franchise language, but the events of Zombie Stage
are not the events of mainline ALNST. See the [Timeline](/timeline/) for details.
