**Sua** in Zombie Stage is the "Sewer" — the AU's nickname for her — a zombified performer whose
arc runs entirely through her fixation on [Mizi](/characters/mizi/). She is the second lead of
Round 1 and the performer of [Candy Scar](/songs/candy-scar/).

## Role in Zombie Stage

- **Round 1 co-lead.** Loses her match against Mizi in the AU's now-notorious auction finale.
- **Function in the AU:** shows how devotion metastasizes when the environment is set up to
  consume both bodies and feelings as product.
- **Relationship to Mizi:** best friend, opponent, and, in Round 1's closing beats, the emotional
  pivot of the entire Round.

## Appearance

Sua's Zombie Stage design plays up the "wet, uncanny" side of the AU — darker palette, visible
decay, the "Sewer" motif on staging. Reference: Fandom
