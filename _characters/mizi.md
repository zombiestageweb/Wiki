**Mizi** in Zombie Stage is the K-pop idol who was zombified and captured — but was famous enough
that the arena kept her "presentable" and put her back on stage. She is the Round 1 headliner
opposite [Sua](/characters/sua/).

## Role in Zombie Stage

- **Round 1 lead.** Performs [Little Bunny](/songs/little-bunny/).
- **Function in the AU:** critique of the idol industry. Mizi's marketability is precisely what
  keeps her alive — and what dooms her, because value here is measured in inventory.
- **Relationship to Sua:** best friend turned Round 1 opponent.

## Appearance

In Zombie Stage art Mizi keeps a stylized version of her idol look — pale, clean silhouette, muted
version of her mainline color palette — with the AU's telltale wound and prosthetic detail. See
the Fandom [Mizi/Zombie Stage gallery](https://alienstage.fandom.com/wiki/Mizi/zombie_stage)
for official art references.
